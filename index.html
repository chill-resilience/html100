SABC

Service Animal Training with Tess in BC (SATTBC) is a training organization in rural British Columbia for working dogs and their humans to learn how to work together effectively to empower people with disabilities to increase their independence. SATTBC was founded in 2021.

SATTBC's dog and trees symbol

The dog and trees symbol represents a dog in a service harness learning in a relaxed natural setting how to reliably assist their disabled person with their activities of daily life, and helping to increase their independence.
