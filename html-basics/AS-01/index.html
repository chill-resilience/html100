Service Animal Training with Tess in BC (SATTBC)

Service dog teams learning together in the woods

SATTBC is a training organization in rural British Columbia for service dogs and their humans to learn how to work together effectively to empower people with disabilities to increase their independence. Service dogs are task trained according to their human's disability needs. SATTBC was founded in 2021.

What is a Service Dog Team?

A service dog team is...

SATTBC's dog / human / trees symbol

The dog / human / trees symbol represents a dog in a service harness learning in a relaxed natural setting how to reliably assist their disabled person with their activities of daily life, and help to increase their independence.
